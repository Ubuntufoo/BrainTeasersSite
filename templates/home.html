Anagram Game

Anagram Game is a fun word game where you have to guess as many anagrams of a starting word as you can in 60 seconds.

  • Guess as many anagrams as you can before time runs out
  • Submit your score, track your progress and join the leader boards!
Math Game

Math Game is a fun game where you have to solve as many equations as you can in 30 seconds.

  • Pick an operator: addition, subtraction, multiplication, or division
  • Solve as many equations as you can before time runs out
  • Submit your score, track your progress and join the leader boards!
